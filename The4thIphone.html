The Fourth Iphone

The fourth iPhone, the iPhone 4, was introduced on June 24, 2010. It marked a major design overhaul with a sleeker, glass-and-metal construction and a Retina display. It was meticulously developed under the leadership of Steve Jobs, emphasizing both form and function, and it introduced features like FaceTime for video calls, making it a significant advancement in the smartphone industry.
