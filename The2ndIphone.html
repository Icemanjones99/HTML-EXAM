The Second Iphone

The second iPhone, known as the iPhone 3G, was released on July 11, 2008. Building upon the success of the original iPhone, it featured faster data speeds with 3G connectivity and was developed through continued collaboration between Apple's design and engineering teams to enhance the smartphone experience.
